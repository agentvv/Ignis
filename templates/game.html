IGNIS





How To Play Ignis

In Ignis you play as a Neanderthal living in a cave. One day lighting strikes outside your cave lighting a fire on the ground. You're curious about the fire and learn to control it by using different materials to grow, change and protect your fire.

Other Neanderthals from the area see your fire and are willing to trade you Sharp Rocks in exchange for a piece of your fire. Your goal is to use these Sharp Rocks to buy items to add to your fire. If your fire grows large enough you win the game!

Watch out for weather events and Neanderthals taking too much of your fire! If your fire gets too small you lose and your fire goes out!

Creating a New Fire

When you create a new fire, your fire defaults to have 1000 size, heat, brightness and smokiness, and 0 protection. You are given 2000 fire stats that you can use to personalize your fire in increments of 100.

When you create a new fire you will see the main game screen, where you can view the current weather, any Neanderthals visiting your fire, your current fire's stats, the buttons to view the shop, inventory, or item info pages, and of course, your current fire. You can also toggle the location of your fire.

Winning and Losing

Your objective is to grow your fire to win the game. You win the game when your fire size reaches 10,000. You lose the game when your fire reaches size 0.

Weather

During the game your fire degrades based on the weather outside every 10 seconds. The cooler, more humid, and windier it is outside the faster you fire will degrade. If a large enough weather event does not happen within 2 minutes, a random weather event will occur of random intensity.

In order to combat the effects of the weather, you can increase your fire's protection stat by purchasing roofs and walls in the shop and using them.

Neanderthals

Other Neanderthals will see your fire and will take 5% of your fire's size in exhange for Sharp Rocks, the in-game currency, at somewhat random intervals. If you have high levels of smokiness and brightness more Neanderthals will take a piece of your fire more often.

If you change the colour of your fire using one of the metals in the shop, you will receive twice as many Sharp Rocks from Neanderthals visiting your fire. The higher the heat and size of your fire, the more Sharp Rocks Neanderthals will give you.

Shop

When you visit the shop you will be able to view three items for sale, and your current Sharp Rocks balance. In the top right hand corner of the image for each shop item, you will see how many Sharp Rocks each item costs. If you want to see how the item changes the attributes of your fire, visit the Item Info page from the main game screen. Click on the button associated with an item in the shop to buy it.

Inventory

When you visit your inventory you will see how many sharp rocks you have, as well as how many of each item you can buy in the shop you have currently. In order to use an item, click the button associated with it, and return to the main game screen to see how your fire has changed.
